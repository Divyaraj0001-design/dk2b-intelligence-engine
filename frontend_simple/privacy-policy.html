Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

Welcome to DK2B (part of The27LINKS Ecosystem). We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of the enterprise data processed through our platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our automated Business Requirements Document (BRD) generation and architectural handoff services.

By using our Scalable Business Logic tools, you trust us with your project communications, and we take that trust seriously.

2. Information We Collect

To provide seamless automated synthesis, our system collects and processes specific categories of data:

  • Project Communications: Data securely ingested from authorized communication channels (e.g., team chat logs, emails, meeting transcripts) provided by your enterprise.
  • Raw Enterprise Data: Documents, software/hardware specifications, IoT data schemas, and robotics project assets uploaded to our platform.
  • Usage Metrics: Technical logs related to API interactions and performance metrics of the automated synthesis engine.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is processed Strictly for enterprise functionality and platform improvement:

  • Automatically generating comprehensive and accurate BRDs from raw input data.
  • Accelerating requirement analysis and mitigating conflicts in complex project lifecycles.
  • Ensuring the security, integrity, and scalability of the DK2B platform.

4. Data Security & Retention

We implement industrial-grade security measures. All project communications and raw data are protected using robust encryption both in transit and at rest. Data ingested for BRD generation is processed in isolated environments.

We retain your data only for as long as your enterprise account is active or as needed to fulfill service agreements. You have the right to request the deletion of ingested raw data at any time.
